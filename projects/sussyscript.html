SussyScript is a static and weakly typed coding language inspired by the popular party game Among Us! SussyScript takes influence from the coding conventions of JavaScript and Python, aimed to introduce coding to crewmates (and also beginner coders familiar with Among Us). Being a language targeted at beginners, SussyScript allows users to learn the basic functions of coding, such as looping and expression creation, with terms and keywords that allude to mechanics in Among Us!
